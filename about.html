About Me

From brand identities to full-blown campaigns, I like to keep things clear, collaborative, and creatively sharp. No bullshit. No ego. Just solid work, built with purpose (and coffee).

I’m a designer with 25 years of experience building brands, shaping strategy, and rolling out campaigns that actually connect with people. I’ve worked across numerous industries, various agencies, and a wide range of roles, and while the titles have changed, the mindset stayed the same: make great work that works.

Over the years, I’ve led teams, pitched ideas, hit tight deadlines, and had more than a few sleepless nights, but learned that the best ideas usually come after the second coffee and the umpteenth sketch.
